about us

Welcome to the official website of Kanabur Diabetes and Health Care Centre Vijayapur. Our centre is established with the aim of reducing the burden of the patient and preventing complications arising from this chronic metabolic disease that is diabetes which is affecting large proportion of population by providing appropriate and timely management of diabetes along with patient centric diet management . We aim at giving quality and comprehensive health care services to our patients with inbuilt diet counselling, lab facilities, eye care, ECG, foot care and many more making our centre a one stop clinic for patients of North Karnataka. We strive to provide our patients evidence based care in diabetes.
